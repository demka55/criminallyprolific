2022–2025

TopicRanker / SERP Gap Analyzer

Acquired by Semrush · Oct 2025

My fourth venture was a direct response to the noise in keyword research. I built and launched it as an app on the Semrush App Center called SERP Gap Analyzer — designed to find easy-to-rank keywords by identifying where competitors were weak. It rapidly became the top-performing app in the entire Semrush App Center, which led to its acquisition by Semrush in October 2025. Semrush was itself acquired by Adobe shortly after.

2021–2023

Smallbiz.Tools

Acquired by Semrush · 2023

I founded my third venture to help entrepreneurs navigate the crowded world of business software. We focused on building a trusted affiliate review site with honest, in-depth reviews — all powered by SEO targeting comparison and alternative keywords, zero paid ads. The site quickly became a go-to resource and was acquired by Semrush in 2023.

2014–2020

JustReachOut

Acquired by SEOJet · 2020, then ChatRank.ai · Jan 2024

I founded JustReachOut to help small businesses handle PR without a PR firm. Over six years I built it into an AI-powered SaaS used by 5,000+ customers including Airbnb and HubSpot. The company was acquired by SEOJet in 2020, then re-acquired by ChatRank.ai in January 2024.

2012–2014

Polar Polls

Acquired by Google · Sept 2014

My journey started as the only marketer on a 5-person team at Polar Polls. I used PR outreach and SEO to grow from zero to 40 million pageviews/month. That growth captured Google's attention, leading to an acquisition in September 2014 — Google's 166th acquisition.
