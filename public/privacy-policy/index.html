This privacy policy describes how Criminally Prolific LLC, a New Hampshire-based LLC ("Company", "us", "our", and "we"), collects, uses, and shares personal information. This Policy covers our apps (YouTube Gap Analyzer, LLM Gap Analyzer, IRS Notice Advisor) and websites (prthatconverts.com and criminallyprolific.com).

1. Data Collection

YouTube Gap Analyzer & LLM Gap Analyzer (Semrush App Center)

Zero Personal Data Collected

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information through these Apps. All user account information is stored and maintained exclusively by Semrush. Your interaction with the App is governed by your existing Semrush account security and privacy protocols.

IRS Notice Analyzer (irs-notice-advisor.vercel.app)

Zero Personal Data Collected

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information — including names, Social Security Numbers, EINs, tax records, or financial data. Any IRS notices you submit are transmitted directly to Anthropic's API for analysis and are not retained by Criminally Prolific LLC. The app automatically redacts personally identifiable information before any analysis occurs. All session data is permanently deleted when you close or refresh the tab.

If you connect your QuickBooks account, your financial data is fetched in real-time solely to provide contextual analysis. We do not store, retain, or share this data. Your use of this app is subject to Intuit's Privacy Policy and Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

prthatconverts.com & criminallyprolific.com

For users of our websites, we collect:

  • Information you give us: Name, email address, and any other information you provide through forms or account registrations.
  • Information automatically collected: Operating system, browser type, pages viewed, time spent on pages, access times, and general usage patterns.
  • Cookies: We use session and persistent cookies to improve your experience and tailor content to your interests.

2. Use of Personal Information

Apps (YouTube Gap Analyzer, LLM Gap Analyzer, IRS Notice Advisor)

Because we do not collect or store personal information via these Apps, we do not use, process, or share any personal information in connection with them. All user data management for the Semrush apps is handled exclusively by Semrush.

Websites (prthatconverts.com & criminallyprolific.com)

We use personal information collected through our websites to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our sites and services
  • Respond to comments and questions and provide customer service
  • Send confirmations, technical notices, and administrative messages
  • Communicate about promotions and news about our products
  • Protect against fraudulent, unauthorized, or illegal activity
  • Provide and deliver the products and services you request

3. Sharing of Personal Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing or commercial purposes.

We only share information in limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party providers who help us operate our Sites (email delivery, hosting). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or to protect our rights.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your information may transfer as part of that business transaction.

4. Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement industry-standard measures:

  • Apps (Semrush): Because we do not collect or store personal data, there is no personal user information held on our servers. Account security is managed by Semrush.
  • IRS Notice Advisor: All data is transmitted encrypted via TLS (HTTPS). Credentials are stored as environment variables on Vercel's secure infrastructure. No personal data is written to any database by Criminally Prolific LLC.
  • Websites: We use standard security technologies and organizational procedures to protect personal information from unauthorized access.

5. Your Choices

  • Marketing Opt-Out: Our marketing emails include instructions to opt-out of future mailings.
  • Personal Information Requests: You may contact us to change your preferences, opt-out of data sharing, or request to update, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Cookie Management: You can remove or reject cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.
  • QuickBooks Connection: You may revoke app access to your QuickBooks data at any time through your QuickBooks account settings.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may change this privacy policy. If we make any changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
